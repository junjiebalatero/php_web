This theme is strongly inspired by the PostNuke default theme

How To:
--------
- Put all the files in your themes folder
and that's all.

Cutomize:
---------
If you want to customize this theme, you'll find eclosed some gif templates to help you. 
Don't forget to change your links between lines 147-152.

if you got trouble with the GetUserTime function, just uncomment or replace line 94
just like this:
// echo strftime(_DATETIMEBRIEF, (GetUserTime(time())));

Be kind, keep the link to our site at the bottom of the theme. 
This will give force to make new themes in the future
